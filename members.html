COREINVEST HOLDINGS is a level 1 BBBEE Afrocentric company formed by professionals specializing in tailoring strategic solutions to existing businesses in distress or those that are at the cross-road in their existence. The combined skills of the CSBS team allows for a robust complimentary executive impact, through their proven track record and many years of experiences across many industries.

CSBS is differentiated from other business rescue and advisory consultancies by its strong focus on corporate governance impact, coupled with financial viability restructuring. More importantly on its ability to conduct early assessments in aid of rescue proceedings, but also to lay strong corporate governance structures going forward, and compliance while saving jobs.
