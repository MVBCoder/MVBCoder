Eco Business Rescue Consortium

Eco Business Rescue Consortium (Eco BRC) is a vehicle that has been formed and born out of the evident need of an integrated entity managed by black Business Rescue Professionals, and aimed at assisting the Private sector, Government, Banks, Chambers, BUSA and the SBI as a result of the COVID-19 and its adverse economic impact on companies.

Driven by a strong team of black Business Rescue Professionals, the Consortium recognizes the urgent need to assist and effectively equip affected business owners by providing them the tools they need for their businessâ€™ survival. Formed as an entity with fundamental credentials in providing specialised services, our professionals boast expertise and experience across a broad range of industries.

Eco BRC recognises the dire financial and economic situation facing SMMEs, and has seen fit to offer Business Consulting assistance to affected entities on terms described below:

payments

Funding from COVID-19 financing earmarked by government budget to costs of assistance

business

Funding from relevant business associations to which SMMEs belong

payment

Nominal fees from SMMEs who can pay

cast_for_education

Stakeholder education through webinars and other media platforms

Eco BRC brings to distressed companies essential expertise in Business Rescue and Turnaround on a broad range of industries with access to various critical expertise, which are essential to ensuring the recovery of financially distressed entities. The Consortium is well placed in terms of providing specialised services, and with members having considerable expertise in a variety of industries.

It is expected that the pandemic will result in long term negative effects on SMMEs and Eco BRC is prepared and equipped to assist distressed entities beyond the immediate phase.
