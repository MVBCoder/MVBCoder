Background

Eco BRC comprises of a team of black Business Rescue and Turnaround professionals who have been actively involved in business turnarounds and rescues in diverse markets over many years.

The Consortium has been formed as an independent body to assist distressed Companies. Our existence as a Consortium is guided by a strong vision of people empowerment, excellence and professionalism. Rooted in the mission of building South Africa, we are committed to helping our country grow through sustainable business practice, entrepreneurship, and equal opportunity for all.

Vision

To be the specialised service provider of choice by consistently meeting the needs of clients by utilising strategic business solutions that yield tangible and positive economic results.

Mission

We are committed to ensuring that in every engagement, we provide solutions that achieve excellent results, and in so doing, to create solutions that ensure that previously distressed businesses are ahead of the competition.

Our values
Transparency-

Eco BRC is an independent consortium of professional service providers. We have no political affiliations and act in the best interest of our clients.

Proactivity-

We do not wait for the storm to hit. We proactively work to provide the best solution, keeping the future of your business in mind.

Excellence-

We are highly qualified to give you expert advise on how to best turn your company around.

Partnership-

We work closely with our clients to achieve optimum results.
